About Me

Education

I studied at the School of Visual Arts in college, majoring in illustration. After graduation, I came to Parsons and am currently studying Design and Technology.

Hobbies and Interests

I have many hobbies, such as painting, playing piano, playing games, visiting exhibitions, and fishing. I am interested in animation design, MG design, and AR and VR design.

Job Interests

In the future, I want to work in the field of art or animation. I aspire to be an art director.

Reflection

Through studying in DT, I learned a lot of techniques. These techniques allowed me to not only stay in the 2D direction, but also explore 3D and interactive techniques, such as p5.js, Arduino, etc., enabling me to realize my wish of converting my 2D works into 3D step by step.

Background Information

My name is Yan Yang, and I was born in Beijing, China. I came to the United States in the 10th grade of high school and spent my high school years in Utah. After that, I came to San Francisco and started the first half of my college life, majoring in illustration. Later, when I was a sophomore, I came to New York and studied illustration at SVA. After graduation, I came to Parsons and studied DT.
